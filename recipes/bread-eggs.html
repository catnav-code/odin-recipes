Bread and eggs

Breakfast consisting of eggs, cheese, multigrain bread and fresh vegetables.

The eggs supply protein, while the multigrain bread supplies fiber, and the fresh vegetables give vitamins. The cheese provides taste, and the green leaves and tomato make the dish healthy.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 slices of multigrain bread
  • One slice cheese
  • half a tablespoon of oil
  • 2 handfuls of leafy greens
  • One medium tomato, sliced
  • Salt, oregano and black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Cook the leafy greens until slightly wilted.
  3. Add the eggs and cook until set.
  4. Place the cheese on top and let it melt.
  5. Toast the bread slices.
  6. Assemble with the eggs, greens, and tomato slices.
  7. Serve Immediately.

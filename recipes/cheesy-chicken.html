Cheesy Chicken!

A flavorful chicken dish cooked with vegetables, cheese, and aromatic Indian spices.

Ingredients:

  • 250g boneless chicken
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped
  • 1 medium capsicum, sliced
  • 1 slice cheese
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chilli powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Cook the onions until softened.
  3. Add the tomatoes and spices and cook until fragrant.
  4. Add the chicken and cook until fully done.
  5. Stir in the capsicum and cook for several minutes.
  6. Place the cheese on top and let it melt.
  7. Serve hot.
